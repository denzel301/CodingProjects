Crypto Token

Like American dollars, tokens represent value, but they are not exactly valuable themselves, in the same way a paper dollar’s value may not be $1. But tokens can be used in transactions for other things. A token differs from a coin in the way it’s constructed within the blockchain of an existing coin like Bitcoin or Ethereum.
